## Brief Bio

I'm a Ph.D. Student at CEA-LIST, Design of Embedded & Autonomous Systems Lab. I'm currently working on the safety improvement of Autonomous Systems based on uncertainty estimation from deep learning components. In particular, I am interested in runtime verification of safety properties based on uncertainty along the software pipeline in autonomous vehicles. I was born and grew up in Cochabamba, Bolivia. I studied electronics engineering at Universidad Privada Boliviana (UPB). I did a master's in electronics, precision systems & telecom at SUPSI, Switzerland, thanks to the RETECA foundation scholarship. During my academic and professional career, I was involved mainly in embedded systems, mobile robots (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles), and machine learning. I was a former lecturer at Universidad Privada Boliviana, a former Data Scientist at Bamboo-Tec, and a former research engineer at Fundacion Jala for the UAS project.
