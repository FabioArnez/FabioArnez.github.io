Fabio Arnez is a research engineer at CEA-List Institute, LSEA, in France, working on trustworthy deep learning for automated systems. He actively participates in industry-driven projects on Trustworthy AI founded by the European Union and the French government. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Paris-Saclay University, France. Previously, he obtained an MSc. in embedded systems and microelectronics from the University of Applied Sciences and Arts of Southern Switzerland (SUPSI). His main research interests are uncertainty estimation in deep neural networks (DNNs), out-of-distribution detection, and runtime monitoring of DNN-based computer vision components from unmanned vehicles and robotics.
